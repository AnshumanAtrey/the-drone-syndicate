## Notes

- IndiaMART prices are often cheapest but quality varies; always verify seller ratings
- Robu.in has the widest drone-specific catalog and competitive prices
- Robocraze is reliable for Jetson/RPi ecosystem but some items frequently go OOS
- Zbotic has the cheapest CNHL LiPo batteries in India
- Quadkart specializes in FPV racing — not ideal for utility/payload drones
- Always check GST inclusion — some sites show ex-GST prices (ElectronicsComp adds 18% GST at checkout)
- Shipping costs vary: Robu.in free above ₹999, Robocraze free above ₹499, Amazon Prime free
